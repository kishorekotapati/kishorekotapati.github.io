About Me:

Hey Guys, I am KK, Founder of zooKKs. I have a Masters Degree in Computer Science. I was a Oracle JD Edwards programmer for 7 years. I immigrated to US 8 years ago and worked for multiple corporate companies in different roles. While I was working, I used to drive long commutes to work for years months. It was very painful but couldn't avoid. I understood its the same with millions of other commuters. So, I started working on zooKKs for the past 1 year while working in my full time job. I used to work around 14 to 16 hours every day. We are focusing on solving daily commuters problem thru carpooling. Our business model is totally different than other carpooling companies. We have built a Minimum Viable Product & it is now in beta testing and also working on 2nd version with advanced features. I strongly believe that I can do lot better if I work full time on zooKKs and I am ready to quit my day job. That's why I am looking for joining Unshackled and I Can’t wait to be part of it. See you soon. Thank you!

One of the most interesting team projects I am involved in a project called “JTD Foundation”. A couple of my acquaintances and I started an Indian NGO, a year ago. We are a group of like-minded individuals who have come together to make a difference and contribute in the field of rural education. We all studied in India, faced hardships all life long, fought our way through the political and corporate ladder, immigrated to the United States with a dream to change our life. We looked for opportunities and being extremely entrepreneurially motivated, we felt it was our duty to give back to the society we came from. One of our main ideas was to prevent rural, economically backward, socially backward students from discontinuing their education due to socio-economic factors. We do this by mentoring a selected group of students. Each mentor has to take personal proctoring responsibility in mentoring and advising the students under them. We do this on a regular weekly or bi-weekly advising sessions, providing guidance in all areas of their life. After these mentoring session, I feel extremely motivated to do better each day, contribute and help others in need of a solution for their problems at hand. This has acted as one of the main catalyst in building zooKKs to solve the long and tiring commute problems of commuters.
